About Bergen.Works

Bergen.Works is an initiative started by a handful of small/medium companies in Bergen passionate about cross-disciplinary innovation. We wanted to create a space where businesses that are through the grind of start-up status and are looking to grow within an energetic and inspiring environment.

We still have some space available and if you want to contribute to the layout and design of the space please get in touch with us as soon as possible. We are currently renovating and building so if you get in touch with us now, you will have the chance to influence your future work environment.
